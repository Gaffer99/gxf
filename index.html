Guide to profitability

Below i'd list what i believe would lead to profitability if you can stick to it

ICT

This is short for Inner Circle Trader, and utilized by many in The Trading community, this style of trading is purely based on price action and incorporates little to no use of trend following or momentum indicators.

Correlation Analysis

Most Forex pairs are correlated(GU/EU & UC/UJ/UCHF), some days price action on your pair may be choppy or difficult to read, this helps you navigate that.

DXY & Treasuries Analysis

With most pairs being paired to the $, understanding the bias for DXY gives you an idea on liquidity target for your pair, with treasuries being inverse of DXY, in an ideal market situation, DXY should pump when Treasuries dump.

Trading Rules

This is what seperates profitable traders from breakeven traders, every system will produce losses, profitable traders stick with their system and respect their rules instead of chasing new systems every losing streak.

Mental Capital

At the end of the day, we are only human, there are time's that despite your knowledge of price action, the market puts you through a blender, when this happens, it's best to take a break from the charts, there will always be more trades down the line.

Risk Management & Trading Hours

This acknowledges that most systems are more profitable at certai hours, and that you'd need to manage the percentage of your balance risked per trade, so that losses don't weigh on you and your account can survive losing streaks.
